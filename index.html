Research Intern

IIT Hyderabad

Dynamic Functional Split in 5G-NR Heterogeneous C-RAN.

The 5G Radio Acess Network (RAN) also called NG-RAN (Next Generation RAN) enables a partially centralized architecture, which enables the deployment of a subset of network functional at a Central Unit (CU). Means, unlike the traditional architecture where all the processing happens at Base Stations (BSs), in NG-RAN only some of the functions reside in Distributed Units (DUs) and the rest of the processing happens at CU. This reduces the operating cost and also enables a centralized coordination system. 3GPP has standardized 8 different possible split options for the 5G C-RAN. In our work, we dynamically change the split option with the variation of user traffic and fronthaul load.
